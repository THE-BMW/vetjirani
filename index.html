About Us

VetJirani bridges the gap between Kenyan farmers and veterinary professionals through a mobile-based platform. Our mission is to provide accessible, expert animal healthcare and empower farmers with timely support and practical tools.

  • Connect farmers with certified vets instantly
  • Enable mobile access to expert animal care
  • Improve livestock health with digital tools
